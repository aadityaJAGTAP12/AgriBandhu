Cotton Farming Guide - India

Cotton (Gossypium spp.) is an important commercial crop in India, grown mainly in kharif season.

LAND PREPARATION
- Deep ploughing (30-40 cm) during summer
- Two cross ploughings to control weeds
- Apply gypsum @ 1-2 tonnes/ha for alkaline soils
- Form ridges and furrows for irrigation

SEED AND SOWING
- Use hybrid seeds like Bt cotton varieties
- Seed rate: 3-4 kg/ha for hybrids, 8-10 kg/ha for desi varieties
- Sowing time: May-June for kharif
- Spacing: 90-120 cm between rows, 45-60 cm between plants
- Sowing depth: 4-5 cm

IRRIGATION SCHEDULE
- Pre-sowing irrigation for seed germination
- First irrigation: 3-4 weeks after sowing
- Subsequent irrigations: Every 10-15 days
- Critical stages: Flowering and boll development
- Avoid water stress during boll formation
- Total water requirement: 700-1000 mm

FERTILIZER MANAGEMENT
- N:P:K ratio 120:60:60 kg/ha
- Apply 50% N and full P,K as basal
- Remaining N in 2-3 splits during growth
- Apply micronutrients (Zn, B, Fe) if deficient
- Use organic manure for soil health improvement

PEST MANAGEMENT
- Major pests: Bollworm, jassids, aphids, whitefly
- Use IPM approach with Bt cotton varieties
- Monitor pest population regularly
- Apply insecticides only when threshold reached
- Encourage natural enemies like ladybird beetles

DISEASE CONTROL
- Common diseases: Bacterial blight, Fusarium wilt
- Use disease-resistant varieties
- Practice crop rotation
- Apply fungicides preventively
- Maintain field sanitation

WEED CONTROL
- Pre-emergence herbicides: Pendimethalin or Fluchloralin
- Post-emergence: Glyphosate for broadleaf weeds
- Manual weeding 30-45 days after sowing
- Inter-cultivation to control weeds

HARVESTING
- First picking: 150-160 days after sowing
- Subsequent pickings: Every 10-15 days
- Harvest when bolls are fully open
- Use mechanical pickers for large farms
- Dry cotton properly before ginning

YIELD EXPECTATIONS
- Hybrid cotton: 25-30 quintals/ha
- Desi cotton: 8-12 quintals/ha
- Factors: Variety, pest management, irrigation, nutrition

CLIMATE REQUIREMENTS
- Temperature: 25-35°C optimum
- Rainfall: 50-100 cm, well distributed
- Soil: Black cotton soil, well-drained
- pH: 6.0-8.0

MONSOON PREPARATION
- Ensure proper drainage during heavy rains
- Monitor for pest resurgence in humid conditions
- Apply fertilizers before rains to prevent leaching
- Protect standing crop from waterlogging
