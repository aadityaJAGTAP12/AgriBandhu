Maize Farming Guide - India

Maize (Zea mays) is an important cereal crop used for food, feed, and industrial purposes.

LAND PREPARATION
- One deep ploughing followed by 2-3 harrowing
- Apply lime @ 2 tonnes/ha for acidic soils
- Form ridges and furrows for rainfed areas
- Ensure proper drainage in heavy soils

SEED SELECTION AND SOWING
- Use hybrid varieties like HQPM-1, Pusa HQPM-5
- Seed rate: 15-20 kg/ha
- Sowing time: June-July for kharif, February-March for rabi
- Spacing: 60x20 cm or 75x15 cm
- Sowing depth: 4-5 cm

WATER MANAGEMENT
- Critical irrigation at knee-high stage
- Second irrigation at tasseling
- Third irrigation at silking/pollination
- Avoid water stress during reproductive stages
- Total water requirement: 500-600 mm

FERTILIZER APPLICATION
- N:P:K ratio 150:75:75 kg/ha
- Apply full P,K and 25% N as basal
- Remaining N in two splits: At knee-high and tasseling
- Apply zinc sulfate @ 25 kg/ha if deficient
- Use organic manure for sustainable production

PEST AND DISEASE MANAGEMENT
- Major pests: Stem borer, armyworm, aphids
- Diseases: Downy mildew, rust, bacterial stalk rot
- Use resistant varieties and crop rotation
- Apply insecticides at economic threshold
- Practice integrated pest management

WEED CONTROL
- Pre-emergence application of Atrazine
- Post-emergence: 2,4-D for broadleaf weeds
- Manual weeding 20-30 days after sowing
- Mulching to suppress weed growth

HARVESTING
- Harvest when kernels are hard and black layer formed
- Moisture content: 20-25% for immediate use
- Dry to 12-14% for storage
- Use combine harvester for large areas

YIELD POTENTIAL
- Average yield: 4-6 tonnes/ha
- High-yielding hybrids: 8-10 tonnes/ha
- Baby corn: Additional 2-3 tonnes/ha

CLIMATE REQUIREMENTS
- Temperature: 20-30°C optimum
- Rainfall: 60-100 cm well distributed
- Soil: Well-drained sandy loam to clay loam
- pH: 6.0-7.5

NUTRIENT MANAGEMENT
- Maize is heavy feeder of nutrients
- Monitor soil fertility regularly
- Apply micronutrients based on soil test
- Use balanced fertilization for quality produce
