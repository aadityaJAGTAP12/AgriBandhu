Soil Health Management for Indian Agriculture

Healthy soil is the foundation of sustainable agriculture and food security.

SOIL TESTING AND ANALYSIS

- Identifies deficiencies and toxicities
- Guides fertilizer recommendations
- Monitors soil health trends
- Prevents over-fertilization

SOIL pH MANAGEMENT

ACIDIC SOILS (pH < 6.5)
- Common in high rainfall areas
- Apply lime @ 2-4 tonnes/ha
- Use dolomite for magnesium deficiency
- Avoid acid-forming fertilizers
- Grow acid-tolerant crops

ALKALINE SOILS (pH > 8.5)
- Common in arid and semi-arid regions
- Apply gypsum @ 2-5 tonnes/ha
- Use acid-forming fertilizers
- Maintain proper drainage
- Grow salt-tolerant crops

SALINITY MANAGEMENT

SALINE SOILS
- Electrical conductivity > 4 dS/m
- Leaching with good quality water
- Gypsum application for sodicity
- Salt-tolerant crop varieties
- Surface drainage improvement

SODIC SOILS
- Exchangeable sodium > 15%
- Gypsum @ 10-20 tonnes/ha
- Green manuring with dhaincha
- Sub-surface drainage
- Amendments like pyrite

ORGANIC MATTER MANAGEMENT

IMPORTANCE OF ORGANIC MATTER
- Improves soil structure and aeration
- Increases water holding capacity
- Provides nutrients slowly
- Enhances microbial activity
- Reduces erosion and runoff

SOURCES OF ORGANIC MATTER
- Farmyard manure: 10-15 tonnes/ha
- Compost: 5-10 tonnes/ha
- Green manure: Dhaincha, sunn hemp
- Crop residues: Rice straw, wheat straw
- Vermicompost: 2-3 tonnes/ha

SOIL STRUCTURE IMPROVEMENT

TILLAGE PRACTICES
- Minimum tillage to preserve structure
- Contour farming on slopes
- Strip tillage for conservation
- Deep tillage for compacted soils
- Subsoiling for hard pans

CROP ROTATION
- Prevents soil-borne diseases
- Maintains soil fertility
- Breaks pest cycles
- Improves soil structure
- Different root depths utilization

COVER CROPS
- Protects soil from erosion
- Adds organic matter
- Fixes nitrogen (legumes)
- Suppresses weeds
- Improves infiltration

MICRONUTRIENT MANAGEMENT

COMMON DEFICIENCIES
- Zinc: Most widespread deficiency
- Iron: In alkaline soils
- Boron: For reproductive development
- Manganese: In alkaline soils
- Copper: In organic soils

CORRECTION METHODS
- Soil application: Zinc sulfate @ 25 kg/ha
- Foliar sprays: 0.5-1% solutions
- Chelated forms for alkaline soils
- Seed treatment for small seeds
- Integrated approaches
